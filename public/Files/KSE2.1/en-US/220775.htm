Integration with SIEM solutions

This section explains how to integrate Kaspersky Scan Engine with SIEM solutions such as Splunk, KUMA, or ArcSight ESM.

To be integrated with a SIEM solution, Kaspersky Scan Engine has to send Syslog events in CEF or SYSLOG format to the SIEM solution.
