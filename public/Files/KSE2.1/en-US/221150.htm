Getting detailed information about events on the dashboard

You can find detailed information about any event on the Kaspersky Scan Engine dashboard, in the Details column of the events table.

The detailed information includes the following data:

  • What action was taken.
  • Whether the action was successful or not.
  • Additional information, if any.

From a security point of view, the most important events are those that contain information about the following actions:

  • User login to the Kaspersky Scan Engine GUI.
  • An attempt to login with incorrect credentials.
  • Turning on or turning off the scan engine.
  • Creating, changing, or deleting a user account.
  • Changing Kaspersky Scan Engine settings.
